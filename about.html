Our Story

A Church Built
on Faith & Love

RCCG Wonderful Mega Church, Ekiti Province 10 is a vibrant, Spirit-filled community located in the heart of Ijero Ekiti, Ekiti State. For over 48 years, we have been committed to transforming lives through the power of the Gospel, equipping believers for purpose, and reaching the lost with the love of Christ.

We believe that every person is created with extraordinary potential. Our ministry is dedicated to helping you discover and walk in that potential through deep worship, sound teaching, and genuine community.

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To make heaven, to take as many people as possible with us and to have a member of RCCG in every family in all nations of the world.

Our Mission

To evangelize to every creature in all nations and to establish Bible-believing churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries.

Our Values

Faith, Holiness, Excellence, Prayer, Community, Service, and the Love of God — these are the pillars that guide everything we do at RCCG Wonderful Mega.
